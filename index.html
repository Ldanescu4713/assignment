Prague

Overview

Prague, the capital of the Czech Republic, is a city steeped in Gothic beauty and Baroque elegance.
Nicknamed "The City of a Hundred Spires", its history dates back over a thousand years.
Once the seat of Holy Roman Emperors, today it’s a vibrant hub of culture, music, and medieval charm.
